Introduction

Mercury_L1_Gitbook

The L1 Dual-Arm Lifting Robot integrates anthropomorphic planetary robotic arms, a mobile chassis, and a lifting mechanism, offering exceptional spatial adaptability and operational flexibility suitable for a wide range of intelligent task scenarios. Its applications span diverse fields—including scientific research and education, healthcare, new retail, and home services—encompassing tasks such as laboratory automation, precision item delivery, merchandise sorting, and item organization and storage, thereby comprehensively empowering research and practice in embodied intelligence.
